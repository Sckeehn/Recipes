# Beef Taco Skillet
## Ingredients
-   1 pound ground beef
-   1 small red onion, chopped
-   1 can (15-1/4 ounces) whole kernel corn, drained
-   10 corn tortillas (6 inches), cut into 1-inch pieces
-   1 bottle (8 ounces) taco sauce
-   1-1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
-   Hot pepper sauce, optional

## Instructions
1. In a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add the corn, tortillas, taco sauce and 1 cup cheese; heat through. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Serve with pepper sauce if desired.
